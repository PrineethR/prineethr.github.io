how high is our map

this exploration looks at the interplay between depth perception and dynamic patterns. this is what I feel I would see if I was a bird flying over an ocean - just jaded and dreaming, with what seems like clouds underneath my wings. the visualization combines multiple layers of noise functions, including Voronoi patterns and Simplex noise. took a while, and 'twas a tad difficult to do this while keeping the code simple and running smoothly (or maybe i'm just not the best programmer around)
